French Macaron

🇫🇷

The French macaron is made using the French meringue method, where egg whites and sugar are whipped together before folding in almond flour. It is delicate and slightly more sensitive to baking conditions.

Italian Macaron

🇮🇹

The Italian macaron is made using the Italian meringue method, where a hot sugar syrup is poured into whipped egg whites. This method creates a more stable meringue, resulting in a slightly chewier texture.

Swiss Macaron

🇨🇭

Swiss macarons are made using the Swiss meringue method, where egg whites and sugar are gently heated over a water bath before whipping. This method produces a smooth and stable meringue, resulting in a slightly denser texture.
